Frequently Asked Questions

Why subscribe to become a Member?
Because you'll get extra extra every day. Only Members get unrestricted access to journalists you know and trust on your favourite devices PLUS there are heaps of unreal +Rewards, like special offers, extras and exclusives, as well as a tonne of discounts and unique experiences to enjoy. They're worth hundreds of dollars a year and are yours as a Member.
Who gets to enjoy Member's benefits and +Rewards?
Do you have a digital subscription with your papers home delivered or just a standalone digital subscription to The Daily Telegraph, Herald Sun, The Courier-Mail or The Advertiser? If so, then you're already a Member and can enjoy all the exclusive benefits and +Rewards that comes with Membership. This however, doesn't include print only Subscribers, or people with a subscription to PerthNow, Supercoach, iTunes, Google Play Newsstand or a foxsports.com.au. And for those with a Teachers or Uni Students subscription, or on a Connected Reader subscription, sorry you're not eligible either. But if you do want to become a Member, please call us on 1300 My News (1300696397).
How long does it take for my home delivery to start?
It could take up to 5 business days before your first paper delivery arrives. But once your delivery is activated, you'll get the next edition, hot off the press.
Can I put my subscription or membership on hold?
The beauty of having a digital subscription or membership is that it travels with you wherever you go. But if your subscription or membership includes home delivery, then you can request to suspend your paper delivery through My Account.
What if I'm out of the home delivery area?
If your address is in a non-serviceable area, we'll contact you to discuss your options.
What sites can you access as part of our news+ network?
Our news+ network is made up of our most popular news sites, like dailytelegraph.com.au, heraldsun.com.au, couriermail.com.au and advertiser.com.au. As a Member, you get access to them all PLUS perthnow.com.au and a subscription to foxsports.com.au.
